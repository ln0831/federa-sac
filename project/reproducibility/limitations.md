# Limitations

- This workspace now has a verified literature starter set, but it does not yet cover the full nearest-neighbor task landscape.
- Existing local result summaries suggest that the current flagship method story may be weak or context-dependent.
- Historical automation logs may point to an older workspace path.
- The completed robustness suite is single-seed and therefore supporting only.
- The live multi-seed custom ablation is still running, so the final mechanism story is not closed.
- The paper direction is intentionally empirical-first and should only pivot back toward a method paper if stronger multi-seed evidence appears.
