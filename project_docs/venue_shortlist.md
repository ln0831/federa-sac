# Venue Shortlist

## Provisional Primary Target

### `Applied Energy`

Fit:

- strongest fit if we keep the paper centered on grid-operational relevance, robustness, topology shift, and evidence-backed evaluation

Why it currently suits this project:

- the present evidence is stronger as an empirical energy-systems paper than as a pure algorithm-superiority paper
- existing results already support a practical story about when topology-aware federated control helps and when it does not

Main risk:

- the manuscript must sound like an energy-systems contribution, not a tooling or benchmark note

## Provisional Backup Target

### `IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid`

Fit:

- strongest direct power-systems fit for active voltage control, graph-aware learning, and topology-shift evaluation

Why it is attractive:

- it aligns closely with the technical area of the main benchmark and the closest literature

Main risk:

- the bar for novelty and system-level significance is higher than the current paper posture comfortably supports

## Other Plausible Q1 Directions

### `IEEE Transactions on Sustainable Energy`

Best when:

- the paper foregrounds DER, inverter coordination, and renewable-grid operation relevance

### `Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence`

Best when:

- the paper leans more into graph-MARL, federated learning, and evaluation rigor than into power-systems framing

### `IEEE Transactions on Power Systems`

Best when:

- stronger system-level novelty and broader validation are added

## Decision Rule

Choose `Applied Energy` if:

- the final paper remains primarily empirical, robustness-aware, and energy-application oriented

Choose `IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid` if:

- we sharpen the contribution into a more authoritative power-systems methodology paper with tighter system insight

## Current Ranking By Practical Fit

1. `Applied Energy`
2. `IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid`
3. `IEEE Transactions on Sustainable Energy`
4. `Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence`
